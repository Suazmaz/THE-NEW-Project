New Castle County

Welcome to New Castle County! New Castle County is the northermost county of Delaware. New Castle County also has a populations of about 570,719 which makes it the most populated county in Delaware. The population of New Castle County is actually greater than both the population of Kent and Sussex County combined. It is also the most economically developed out of the three counties of Delaware. New Castle County contains about 60% of Delaware's popluation which is around 989,948. Wilmington, which is located inside the county is the states most populous city.
