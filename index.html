Delaware is one of the 50 states in the United States of America. Delaware has three counties which are Sussex, Kent, and New Castle County and Delaware was also the first state to ratify the constitution, so it is also called the first state. Delaware's state capital is Dover. The state bird is the Blue Hen. Delaware has a population of around 1,018,396. This makes Delaware one of the top states in the U.S. with the highest population density per square mile.
