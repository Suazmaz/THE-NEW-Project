Welcome to Sussex County! Sussex County is a county in Delaware, United States. Sussex County is one of three counties in Delaware and is also the most southern county in Delaware. Sussex County has a population of around 249,447 people. This makes Sussex County the second-most populous county in Delaware. The county is well known for its agriculture as well as its beaches. According to datausa.io, 12.1% of the population in Sussex County, Delaware, is in poverty or lives below the poverty line. Fun fact: Lewes, Delaware, is the oldest town in Sussex County.
